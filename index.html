About Me

I am a Research Scholar in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad. My research focuses on Blockchain Technology for Intelligent Transportation Systems, with an emphasis on securing and validating messages exchanged within ITS networks. Additionally, I have worked on attribute-based cryptographic key agreement protocols.
